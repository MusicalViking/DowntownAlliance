Our Story

Who We Are

The Augusta Downtown Alliance is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant and thriving downtown Augusta. As the heart of Maine's capital city, we work to enhance the economic and cultural vitality of our downtown district. As a Main Street America™ Accredited program, the Augusta Downtown Alliance is part of a national network of more than 1,200 neighborhoods and communities who share both a commitment to creating high-quality places and to building stronger communities through preservation-based economic development.

Located in one of the Top 50 Small Towns in America according to Livability.com, Downtown Augusta proudly joins the ranks of two other prominent Maine communities as one of only three cities in the state to be listed. Our downtown offers an eclectic mix of historic charm and modern amenities, making it a destination for residents and visitors alike.

Situated in a sloping valley along the Kennebec River, Downtown Augusta offers an eclectic mix of old and new, perfect for either a family outing or a night out on the town.

As a largely volunteer organization composed of merchants, developers, and stakeholders, the Augusta Downtown Alliance (ADA) is thrilled to represent this diverse community, and remains committed to further developing it by fostering business growth, improving the region's quality of life, and promoting beautification.

Proudly partnered with Main Street America, ADA is pleased to follow the tried and tested Four Point Approach of Organization, Promotion, Economic Restructuring and Design that has worked so well in other Main Street communities throughout America.

So come and join ADA so you can see for yourself just what makes Downtown Augusta so uniquely special.

2016

First annual River of Trees, November 25, 2016 - Now an annual holiday staple, River of Trees involves the community, a fun atmosphere, and serves as one of the largest fundraisers for the Augusta Downtown Alliance.

2018

First Trick or Treat on Water Street - October 31, 2018 - Previously called "Howls and Hounds" 2018 was the year this event became "Trick or Treat on Water Street". This is now one of the Augusta Downtown Alliance's flagship events!

2019

Two-Way Traffic Approved on Water Street - August, 2019.

2021

T-Mobile Hometown Grant Sturgeon Project, 2021: Augusta Downtown Alliance, through the generosity of T-Mobile’s Hometown Grant, installed 26 fiberglass sturgeon on Water Street for 3 years. In 2024, the sturgeon statues were auctioned off, and the proceeds were split with the artists who painted and created them.

Our Mission

The Augusta Downtown Alliance fosters a vibrant, welcoming community by enhancing the economic, cultural, and social vitality of downtown Augusta. We achieve this through strategic partnerships, innovative programming, and preservation of our historic downtown character.

Our Vision

We envision a downtown Augusta that is the thriving heart of our region—a welcoming, walkable, and economically vibrant destination where people live, work, shop, dine, and gather to celebrate our unique community.
